Mars Rover Kata

Open up the console and play me!

Start by typing any letter :)

These are the commands:

  • R is for Right
  • L is for Left
  • F is for Forward
  • B is for Backward
  • S is to define "South" as the direction
  • N is to define "North" as the direction
  • W is to define "West" as the direction
  • E is to define "East" as the direction
